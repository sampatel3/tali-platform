01

About the role

We're hiring a Senior AWS Platform Engineer to own the cloud platform that runs our regulated banking workloads across the GCC. You'll lead IaC, CI/CD and the move toward AI-assisted infrastructure on Bedrock and SageMaker.

02

What you'll do

  • Design and operate multi-account AWS landing zones with Terraform.
  • Build and harden CI/CD for platform and ML services.
  • Introduce Bedrock / SageMaker patterns for internal AI tooling.
  • Partner with security on SOC2 / regional compliance.
03

Recruiter requirements

  • 8+ years production AWS, with at least 2 at senior/staff level.
  • Deep Terraform / IaC; multi-account experience.
  • Exposure to Bedrock or SageMaker (must-have).
  • FinTech / banking domain strongly preferred.
