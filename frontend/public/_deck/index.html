03·What Taali is

A hiring agent, and an AI-native assessment. Built inside a placement agency.

Taali works each live mandate end-to-end — picks up new applicants the moment they land, clears the deterministic screening, ranks the field, and queues the calls that need a human. Every shortlisted candidate sits a real, in-browser work sample with AI in their hands. You hand the client evidence, not a hunch.

01SCREEN
The agent runs your pipeline.
One orchestrator plus a deterministic policy works every active role in real time — pre-screen, CV↔JD scoring, ranking, dedupe across your ATS. Your consultants spend their hours on the shortlist, not the slush pile.
Always-on per active role Two-way ATS sync Human sign-off on every advance / reject
02PROVE
Real IDE. Real AI. Real evidence.
The candidate works in the exact environment they'd use on day one — editor, terminal, repo, Claude side panel. We capture every move and score a 5-axis rubric, including how well they work with AI — the dimension your clients now hire for, and nobody else measures.
5-axis · coding · working with AI · problem-solving · independence · communication Live fraud + proxy-candidate detection

Every verdict is evidence-linked. And it learns from you.
The agent makes the call; you see exactly why — which signals, which rubric version, which clip of evidence. Defensible to your client's hiring committee, and in regulated sectors, to an auditor. Override it and it retrains overnight.
