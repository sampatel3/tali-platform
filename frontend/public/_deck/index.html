03·What Taali is

A hiring agent, and an AI-native assessment. You stay in control.

Taali works each live mandate end-to-end — picks up applicants the moment they land, scores and ranks them, and surfaces the calls that need a human on your home hub. Ask it in plain English to tailor a role and pull the right shortlist. Every candidate you put forward can sit a real, in-browser work sample with AI in their hands.

01SCREEN & SEARCH
The agent runs your pipeline.
One orchestrator plus a deterministic policy works every active role in real time — pre-screen, CV↔JD scoring, ranking, dedupe across your ATS. Then just ask: "top 5 with payments and AI experience" — and re-tune the bar on the fly.
Always-on per active role Conversational search + role tailoring
02PROVE
Real repo. Real Claude. Real evidence.
The candidate works in the exact environment they'd use on day one — a repo, an editor, and Claude alongside. We capture every move and score a 5-axis rubric, including how well they work with AI — the dimension your clients now hire for, and nobody else measures.
5-axis · coding · working with AI · problem-solving · independence · communication Live fraud + proxy-candidate detection
03CONTROL
Human sign-off on every call. And it learns from you.
Advance and reject decisions queue on your home hub with the agent's reasoning, score and evidence. You approve, override or reject — it never acts alone. Every verdict is rule-traced and auditable, and your overrides retrain the policy overnight.
HITL gate on every advance / reject Evidence-linked, exportable, FS-defensible
