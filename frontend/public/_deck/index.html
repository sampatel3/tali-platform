02·The problem

AI has made hiring easier to enter — and harder to trust.

More applications create more recruiter work, while the signals used to make decisions reveal less about who can actually perform.

01 · Volume
More
volume.

AI-tailored applications can be produced at scale. Strong candidates disappear inside manual triage, duplicate records and inconsistent screening.

02 · Signal
Less
signal.

A CV records claims. Legacy tests often remove the AI tools people use at work, so they miss who can direct, challenge and verify AI while still delivering.

The new hiring stack needs both.Automation to run the funnel — and trustworthy evidence to support the decision.
