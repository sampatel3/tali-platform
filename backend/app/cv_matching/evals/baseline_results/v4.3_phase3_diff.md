Per role family, plot the distribution of `calibrated_p_advance` for
golden cases. A bimodal distribution (cluster near 0, cluster near 1)
indicates calibrators are correctly separating advance from reject.
A unimodal lump near 0.5 indicates the calibrator is too weak — fit
on more override data.
